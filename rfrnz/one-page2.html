Who we are

We are a Legal Tech startup based in Munich and Berlin. Our team combines extensive experience in the legal sector with expertise in state-of-the-art Natural Language Processing and Machine Learning.

What we do

We build a contract analysis tool for lawyers and other legal professionals who need to review contractual documents. Using state-of-the-art Natural Language Processing and Machine Learning algorithms, we are automatizing contract analysis. Our customers are able to save 40-60% of their time when reviewing either single or a large number of contracts. At the same time, the results contain less errors – our software won't get tired or distracted.
