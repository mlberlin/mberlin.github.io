Who we are

We are an award-winning Legal Tech company based in Munich and Berlin. Our team combines extensive experience in the legal sector with expertise in state-of-the-art Natural Language Processing and Machine Learning.

What we do

We build a contract analysis tool for lawyers and other legal professionals who need to review contractual documents. Using state-of-the-art Natural Language Processing and Machine Learning algorithms, our solution extracts key information, relevant clauses, or concepts from multiple contract types. The system is language-agnostic and can be used with pre-trained contract clauses or user-specific content.
